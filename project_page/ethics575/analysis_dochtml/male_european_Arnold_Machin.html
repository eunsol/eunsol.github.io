    19After his great postal and mint triumphs of the 1960 's , Sir Arnold continued to produce portraits of the royal family , including the designs for the commemorative coins issued for the royal silver wedding anniversary in 1972 and the 25th anniversary of the Queen 's coronation in 1977 .
    20As a hobby , Sir Arnold took up more ambitious sculpting , working not in terracotta but in landscapes , creating at his home near Eccleshall a large garden of paths , grottoes , waterfalls and fountains .
