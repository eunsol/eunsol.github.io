    2In 1970 , he proved the scheme analog of the Noether -- Enriques -- Petri theorem , which later allowed him to solve a Schottky-type problem for the polarized Prym varieties , and to prove the existence of a line on smooth Fano varieties .
    3In 1983 , Shokurov 's paper Prym varieties : theory and applications was published .
    4In 1984 he published a paper titled On the closed cone of curves of algebraic 3-folds where he proved that the negative part of the closed cone of effective curves on an algebraic 3-fold -LRB- with admissible singularities -RRB- is locally polyhedral .
    5A bit later , in 1985 , Shokurov published a paper titled The nonvanishing theorem , which became a cornerstone for the whole MMP as it was used in the proofs of such fundamental theorems as the Cone theorem and the Semi-ampleness theorem .
    6Later on , in 2001 , Shokurov announced the proof of the existence of 4-dimensional log flips , whose complete version appeared in two books : Flips for 3-folds and 4-folds and Birational geometry : linear systems and finitely-generated algebras .
