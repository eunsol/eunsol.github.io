    1Ma Zhanshan According to some western sources Ma Zhanshan was Born in Liaoning in 1887 .
    2He was promoted to Guard Monitor of the 4th Security Guard Battalion for his good marksmanship and equestrianism , by Wu Junsheng , Commander of Tianhou Road Patrol and Defense Battalion of Mukden in 1908 .
    3In 1913 , Ma was appointed as Major and Company Commander of 3rd Company , 3rd Regiment , 2nd Brigade of the Central Cavalry Army in the Army of the Republic of China .
    4In 1920 , he was promoted to colonel and followed his patron , warlord Wu Junsheng .
    5Zhang Xueliang telegraphed the Nanjing Government to ask for instructions , and then appointed Ma Zhanshan to act as Governor and Military Commander-in-chief of Heilongjiang Province on October 10 , 1931 .
    6He flew to Shenyang in January 1932 , where he attended the meeting that founded the puppet state of Manchukuo .
    7On 1 April 1932 , he led his troops from Qiqihar supposedly on a tour of inspection .
    8Other sources do not mention this doubtful participation of Ma Zhanshan in this war , since he was a Commander in Chief of Cavalry in the National Revolutionary Army in China in 1937 .
    9He crossed over to the Communist Party in January 1949 after persuading General Fu Zuoyi to allow the city to be taken bloodlessly by the Communists .
    10After the founding of the People 's Republic of China , Chairman Mao Zedong invited him to attend the Chinese People 's Political Consultative Conference in June 1950 , but he failed to attend because of illness and he died the same year on November 29 in Beijing .
